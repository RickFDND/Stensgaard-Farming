About us

Stensgaard Farming is a family-run farm located in the rolling countryside of Dronninglund, Northern Denmark. We grow high-quality potatoes, raise healthy dairy cattle, and cultivate beautiful peonies that reflect the natural beauty of our surroundings. With deep roots in Danish farming traditions, we combine experience and care with modern, sustainable methods to ensure quality in everything we do. Every crop and every animal is tended with respect for nature, our land, and future generations. At Stensgaard Farming, we believe that true quality comes from dedication, hard work, and a genuine love for what we do.
