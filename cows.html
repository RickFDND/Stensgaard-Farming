Our Jersey Cows

At Stensgaard Farming, the Jersey cow is at the very center of our work. With their golden-brown coats, gentle nature, and compact build, they bring both charm and efficiency to our farm. Jerseys are prized for their rich, creamy milk, naturally higher in fat and protein, making it perfect for high-quality dairy products such as cheese and yogurt. At the same time, their smaller size and resilience make them a sustainable choice, requiring less feed while maintaining strong health and longevity. For us, Jerseys represent the perfect combination of beauty, quality, and sustainability.

The Benefits of the Jersey Breed

    Exceptional milk quality

  • Jersey milk stands out with naturally higher levels of fat and protein. This makes it ideal for creating rich cheeses, creamy yogurts, and indulgent ice cream.

    • Efficient and sustainable

  • Jerseys are smaller than many other dairy breeds, meaning they require less feed while still producing milk of outstanding quality. Their efficiency contributes to a lower environmental footprint.

    • Healthy and resilient

  • Jerseys are known for their strong health, adaptability, and long productive lifespan. These traits ensure a stable, healthy herd for many years.
How We Care for Our Jerseys At Stensgaard Farming, we believe that animal welfare is the foundation of quality. Our Jersey cows are given the care, nutrition, and space they need to thrive. By focusing on their well-being, we ensure that every liter of milk is produced by cows that are both healthy and content. A Herd to Be Proud Of For us, Jerseys represent more than just dairy production—they embody our values of sustainability, quality, and respect for nature. With every cow, we continue to build a herd we can be truly proud of, and milk we are proud to share.
